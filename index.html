Welcome

to Bread4World

Bread for world is an international organization. In 1984, the movement started in a community in America as an NGO which later annexed to Europe (Uk and Greece) and Africa (Cameroon and South Africa).

The company main aim and initial objective is to shelter the less privilege, feed the orphans and widows, attend to the needy, the sick and injured.

History

In 1995, Bread for world came to Africa with her former agenda until 2016 when they ventured into multilevel marketing in South Africa, which serves as a trusted ”PLAN B”, for more food in your kitchen and financial upliftment.
Therefore, this is the time for us to pitch our tent in the biggest black Nation, Nigeria.

Our Services

-Out door services and promotion of goods and services of different brands E.g; food, beverages, toiletries, home appliances, materials, etc. -Humanitarian services, Eg; free medical eye care. -Event planning, Interior decoration -Skill acquisition programs
E.g; Vocational skills, bakery, bead-making, soap-making, make-up, etc.
