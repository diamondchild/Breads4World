QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions(?)

1. What is Bread for World multi level marketing/network marketing?
Bread for world is an international organization. In 1984, the movement started in a community in America as an NGO which later annexed to Europe (Uk and Greece) and Africa (Cameroon and South Africa).
The company main aim and initial objective is to shelter the less privilege, feed the orphans and widows, attend to the needy, the sick and injured.


2. How much will it cost me to get started?
It will just cost you Thirty-Two Dollars($32) to start

3. Will I get the training and support I need?
Absolutely. BreadsForWorld has a well-established training and support infrastructure in place around the country - you can simply plug into it. Networking Marketing is also a team effort, it is a classic win-win situation. if we help you become successful and make money, then we benefit too.

4. Will I have to quit my present job?
No. Over 90% of people start their Networking Marketing career on a very part-time basis. 7-10 hours a week is normal. As your Network Marketing income grows, build up a reserve of funds for yourself. Then, when your Networking income outstrips your salary, you can re-evaluate your position. That way you have security and an income while you learn and develop your new business.

5. How much time does it take?
There is no precise answer to this buy you should plan to spend at least 7 - 10 hours a week during the first few months. You can devote more time if your circumstances allow. In the first few months, most of your time is used to learn about the business, to develop your customer base, and to identify a few key people who want to join you team and commit to doing what you are doing. In the later months, your time is leveraged through the efforts of your whole team. As your team grows you begin to build up your income base.

6. How much money can i expect to make?
This is your own business - so you decided how much money you want to make. Significantly larger incomes arise from building a team of people who are doing the same as you. There is no limit to the size of income you can achieve.

7. Do I Have to Know Lots Of People?
No. Although it's surprising just how many people we all do know without realizing it. You only need a few good people atleast two persons who are serious about changing their lives and who have a real desire to make it happen. Unlike a traditional job, everyone is a potential candidate for your business.
